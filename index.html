About Me

I'm a full-stack web developer based in Toronto, Canada. As a graduate of the Full-Time Immersive Web Development Bootcamp at Hacker You, I work in JavaScript -- specifically React, Node, and ES6 -- HTML, and CSS. From components to mixins, I write clean, DRY, modular code in order to create compelling and accessible user experiences.

I studied Linguistics and English Literature at Concordia University, where I developed a love of elegant logic, creative problem solving, and clear communication. Before I began coding, I founded and produced Raconteurs Storytelling, a live storytelling series, for which I organized over 70 live events, produced two radio specials for CBC, and made a 6-episode TV series for Bell Fibe!

When I'm not coding, I like to spend some time away from screens. I enjoy hiking and cross-country skiing, and am also an avid reader.
